After i3 and more recently awesome3.4, I finally moved to bspwm.

Here are two screenshots of what my desktop looked like under awesome:

I recently stumbled upon this very nice subreddit unix porn. On it I came across some great desktops using that new window manager bspwm with lemonbuddy bar and decided to give it a try. Here's how my desktop now looks like:

I based my configs on dotfiles found on unix porn and bar's configurations from the creator of lemonbuddy.

Besides its beauty, bspwm is definitely a very nice tiling window manager, the simplicity of its configuration, and the fact it is so easily customizable makes it the perfect wm for tiling wm's lovers !
